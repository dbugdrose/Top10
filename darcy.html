Darcy's Top 10

1. Anything - Adrienne Lenker

Makes me feel feelings when I need to not be numb. Beautiful lyrics, gentle melody.

2. Runaway - Aurora

Gorgeous vocals, everything sung by Aurora feels like magic.

3. Creature - Half Alive

Very stimmy song, makes my brain calm.

4. Karma Chameleon - Culture Club

Classic 80s sound, hard to not be happy or sing along while listening.

5. The Sound of Silence - Disturbed

Haunting vocals that make me feel deeply. Takes me out of my thoughts to just exist and experience.

6. Murder Song - Aurora

Again, her voice is so beautiful. Everything she sings I love and appreciate. Also the lyrics of this resonate with me.

7. Why Do You Feel So Down? - Declan McKenna

Upbeat poppy sound, depressing lyrics, the whole enchilada. Ya know.

8. Home - Edward Sharpe & the Magnetic Zeros

It's fun and joyous and folksy. Love everything about it.

9. Habit - Still Woozy

This song makes me feel chill and relaxed, it’s fantastic.

10. Sorry - Dodie

I love everything Dodie puts out. I'm moved by her high, clear, feminine vocals and her emotional delivery.
