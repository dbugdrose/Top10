Brandon's Top 10

1. Burn It All Down - PVRIS

This song was a surprise to see when it dropped at the time as it featured one of my favorite artists; Denzel Curry, and this song was also being performed at League Worlds 2021 Opening Ceremony.

2. Kazino - BIBI

I enjoy BIBI’s music and her vocal range tha she has. This song is no exception, her singing is amazing to listen to in this song. This is one of my personal favorite songs of hers that I find myself coming back to for the catchy hook.

3. Bandoleros - Don Omar

This song was a classic and is only second to the other song in this soundtrack by the Teriyaki Boyz. Both the lyrics and sound of the song are catchy. I chose this song as it features another big name in Reggaeton, Tego Calderon, and he complements the song well with his rapping parts.

4. Pa'lla Voy - Marc Anthony

This is such a catchy song that it’s played a lot at Latin clubs and parties as people enjoy dancing to this song. I like the energetic sound that this song brings with its catchy hook and the use of real instruments in the song.

5. Salio El Sol - Don Omar

I have a ton of nostalgia for this song as I heard this song so often growing up. The sound of the song and lyrics are catchy and easy to follow.

6. More - K/DA

This song is sung by a fictional group that is composed of playable characters from League of Legends. The surprising part is that the music video for this song was given a lot of budget and is done very well. The vocals are nice and all flow smoothly throughout the song.

7. Armageddon - Aespa

I like the intro to this song as it begins as well as the catchy hook that they use through the song. All members of the group were great in their parts of the song. The bang-chitty-bang part is also really catchy to hum/sing along to.

8. Ella Me Levanto - Daddy Yankee

This song was another one of those that I heard often when I was growing up. It was a song that was often played at parties. Once you heard that intro with the trumpets, you would see people standing up to go to the dance. The lyrics are catchy and the melody is energetic.

9. Sheesh - BABYMONSTER

This song has a great blend of singing with rap that transitions well with the catchy hook.

10.Howling - XG

The reason I chose this song is for the same reason that I enjoy listening to the group, their rap. Each member’s part of the song is well done and blends smoothly behind a catchy sound. I enjoy listening to this song often for the rapping and catchy hook they used of a wolf howl to match the theme of the song.
