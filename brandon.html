Brandon's Top 10

1. Burn It All Down - PVRIS feat. Denzel Curry

This song was a surprise to see when it dropped at the time as it featured one of my favorite artists; Denzel Curry, and this song was also being performed at League Worlds 2021 Opening Ceremony.

2. Up - NAV

This song has nostalgia for me as it was big during my years in high school and it being played everywhere at school or in other students' cars as they drove by.

3. Bandoleros - Don Omar

This song was a classic and is only second to the other song in this soundtrack by the Teriyaki Boyz. BOth the lyrics and sound of the song are catchy

4. Pa'lla Voy - Marc Anthony

This is such a catchy song that it's played a lot at Latin clubs and parties as people enjoy dancing to this song.

5. Salio El Sol - Don Omar

I have a ton of nostalgia for this song as this song was played a lot in my household growing up. The song is very catchy and energetic.

6. Colmillo - Tainy

I always find myself coming back to this song as it features a lot of big Latin names and incorporates all of their styles into one song. It has a unqiue song compared to what they've done in their past works.

7. Armaggedon - Aespa

I like the intro to this song as it begins as well as the catchy hook. All members for the group were great in their parts of the song.

8. Ella Me Levanto - Daddy Yankee

This song was another one of those that I heard often when I was growing up. It was a song that was often played at parties. The lyrics are catchy and the melody is energetic.

9. Bartender - T-Pain

This song is just a classic that almost everyone knows. This was around the time when T-Pain was dominating the charts and released as well as featured in some of the biggest songs.

10. Howling - XG

The reason I chose this song is for the same reason that I enjoy listening to the group, their rap. Each member's part of the song is well done and blends smoothly behind a catchy sound.
